Frequently Asked Questions About Kashmir Honeymoon Packages

How many days are ideal for a Kashmir honeymoon?

A 5 Nights / 6 Days honeymoon package is ideal to comfortably explore Srinagar, Gulmarg, Pahalgam and Sonmarg while enjoying romantic experiences and leisure time.

What is included in your honeymoon packages?

Our honeymoon packages can include private cab service, hotel or houseboat accommodation, sightseeing, airport transfers and optional honeymoon decorations.

Which is the best season for a honeymoon in Kashmir?

Spring (March–May) offers tulip gardens and pleasant weather, while winter (December–February) is perfect for couples who want to enjoy snowfall and snow-covered landscapes.

Can we customize our honeymoon package?

Yes. Every honeymoon package can be customized according to your budget, preferred destinations, hotel category and travel dates.

Best Time for a Kashmir Honeymoon

Kashmir is a year-round destination with every season offering a unique experience. Choose the best time according to your travel plans and interests.

🌸 Spring Season (March - May)

Perfect time to visit Tulip Garden, enjoy pleasant weather and explore the beautiful landscapes of Kashmir.

☀️ Summer Season (June - August)

Ideal for sightseeing, family holidays and exploring Srinagar, Gulmarg, Pahalgam and Sonmarg.

🍂 Autumn Season (September - November)

Enjoy colorful landscapes, peaceful surroundings and beautiful views of Kashmir valley.

❄️ Winter Season (December - February)

Best time for snowfall lovers to experience Gulmarg snow activities, winter landscapes and Kashmir's magical beauty.
